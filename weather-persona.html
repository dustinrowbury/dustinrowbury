Site Purpose


This site will provide fast service to those who visit it by showing them the forcasted weather simply and quickly.


Audience Description


Visitors will mostly be 18 and older, they will be looking to see what the weather will be like because of driving plans or other traveling purposes. The most sought after information will be temperature and a summary of the weather condition.
